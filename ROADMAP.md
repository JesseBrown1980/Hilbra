# Hilbra Roadmap — from a 2‑colony link to a global internet of fabrics

Eight gated phases. Every gate is **`auto_fire_allowed = false`** until a human operator T0 + (for canon/substrate changes) a quorum cosign. Nothing fires to real metal or real traffic without passing the gate before it. Constraints are hard laws: **Rust‑canonical, pixels‑before‑GPU, key‑off‑wire, web‑of‑trust‑not‑CA.**
